<p><p>Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) are precision instruments used to measure an object's physical geometrical characteristics. The market comprises several types: Bridge CMMs, known for their high accuracy and stability, are ideal for larger components. Articulated Arms provide flexibility and portability for on-site inspections. Horizontal Arm CMMs offer versatility in measuring small to medium-sized parts, typically used in production environments.</p></p>
