<p><strong>The X Ray Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog X-Ray Machine</li><li>Digital X-Ray Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray machine market is categorized into analog and digital types. Analog X-ray machines use film to capture images, providing a traditional method of imaging but requiring chemical processing and often resulting in longer wait times for results. In contrast, digital X-ray machines utilize electronic sensors to capture images, offering faster processing, enhanced image quality, and reduced radiation exposure. Digital systems also enable easier storage, sharing, and analysis of images, making them increasingly popular in modern medical settings.</p></p>
