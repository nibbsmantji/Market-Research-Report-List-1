<p><p>Water Activated Tape (WAT), also known as gummed tape, is a type of adhesive tape that requires water to activate its adhesive properties. It is commonly used in packaging for its strong bonding capabilities, tamper-evident features, and eco-friendly nature, as it is often made from paper and starch-based adhesives. WAT is widely used in industries such as e-commerce, logistics, and manufacturing due to its durability and ability to securely seal heavy-duty boxes.</p><p>The Water Activated Tape Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period. The market is also benefiting from advancements in adhesive technologies and the development of biodegradable and recyclable WAT products.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the adoption of water-activated tapes in the food and beverage industry for their non-toxic properties and the growing preference for lightweight and high-strength packaging materials. The Asia-Pacific region is expected to witness significant growth due to expanding industrial and e-commerce sectors.</p></p>
