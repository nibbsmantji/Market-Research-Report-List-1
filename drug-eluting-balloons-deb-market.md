<p><p>Drug Eluting Balloons (DEB) are advanced medical devices used primarily in the treatment of cardiovascular diseases, particularly in angioplasty procedures. These balloons are coated with drugs that are released during the inflation of the balloon, targeting the affected arterial tissue to prevent restenosis (narrowing of the artery again). The innovative approach of DEB combines the mechanical advantages of balloon angioplasty with the pharmacological benefits of drug delivery.</p><p>The Drug Eluting Balloons (DEB) Market is poised for significant expansion, driven by an increase in cardiovascular diseases, a rise in awareness regarding minimally invasive procedures, and technological advancements in medical devices. Healthcare professionals prefer DEBs for their ability to enhance treatment efficacy while minimizing complications associated with conventional stents.</p><p>Market growth is further supported by the aging population, increasing prevalence of obesity and diabetes, and rising healthcare expenditures.
