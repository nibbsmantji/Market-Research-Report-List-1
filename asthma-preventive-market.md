<p><p>The asthma preventive market is segmented primarily into medications like Sodium Cromolyn and Prednisolone. Sodium Cromolyn is a mast cell stabilizer that helps prevent asthma attacks by inhibiting the release of histamine and other inflammatory mediators, often used for long-term control. Prednisolone, a corticosteroid, reduces inflammation and suppresses immune responses, providing effective short-term management of asthma exacerbations. Both types target different aspects of asthma management, offering complementary approaches to improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
