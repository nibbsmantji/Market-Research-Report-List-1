<p><p>The ultrasound transducer market consists of three main types: linear array, convex array, and phased array transducers. Linear array transducers produce high-resolution images ideal for vascular and superficial examinations. Convex array transducers offer broader imaging angles, making them suitable for abdominal and pelvic scans. Phased array transducers are compact, allowing for real-time imaging in cardiology and neonatal applications. Each type serves specific diagnostic needs, driving their demand in the medical imaging landscape.</p></p>
