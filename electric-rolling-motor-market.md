<p><p>The Electric Rolling Motor market is categorized into two primary types: non-torque type and torque type. Non-torque type motors are designed for applications that require consistent speed and smooth operation without significant load variations, making them ideal for moderate-speed applications. In contrast, torque type motors focus on delivering high torque at lower speeds, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications that demand powerful performance for tasks like lifting or moving heavy loads. Each type addresses specific operational requirements in various industries.</p></p>
