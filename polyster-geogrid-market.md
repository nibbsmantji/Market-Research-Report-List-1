<p><p>The Polyester Geogrid market comprises two main types: Regular-Tow Geogrid and Large-Tow Geogrid. Regular-Tow Geogrid is characterized by its smaller, closely spaced fibers, providing enhanced tensile strength and stability for various applications like road construction and soil reinforcement. In contrast, Large-Tow Geogrid features thicker fibers, facilitating quicker installation and offering cost-effective solutions for large-scale projects. Both types serve critical roles in improving spatial stability and load distribution in civil engineering and infrastructure developments, catering to diverse project needs.</p></p>
