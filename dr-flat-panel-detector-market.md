<p><strong>The DR Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect FPD</li><li>Direct FPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DR Flat Panel Detector market comprises two primary types: Indirect FPD and Direct FPD. Indirect FPDs utilize a two-step process, converting X-rays into light using a scintillator, which is then detected by a photodiode array, making them suitable for various imaging applications. In contrast, Direct FPDs convert X-rays directly into electrical signals through semiconductor materials, offering improved image quality and faster processing times. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic imaging technologies across healthcare facilities.</p></p>
