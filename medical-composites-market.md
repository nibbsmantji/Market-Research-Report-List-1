Carbon composites are valued for their strength and lightweight properties, making them ideal for prosthetics and surgical tools. Ceramic composites offer exceptional biocompatibility and wear resistance, often utilized in dental and orthopedic applications. Glass fiber composites provide good mechanical strength and flexibility, commonly found in medical devices and structural implants. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing medical technology and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
