<p><strong>The Subtitling and Transcription Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Muxed Subtitles</li><li>Burned Subtitles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subtitling and transcription service market includes various formats such as muxed subtitles and burned subtitles. Muxed subtitles, or soft subtitles, are encoded separately from the video and can be toggled on or off by the viewer, allowing for user flexibility. In contrast, burned subtitles, or hard subtitles, are permanently embedded into the video, which makes them always visible and suitable for platforms where subtitle control is limited. Both formats cater to diverse audience needs and viewing environments.</p></p>
