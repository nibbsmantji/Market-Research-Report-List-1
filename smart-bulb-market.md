<p><p>The smart bulb market consists of several key types, including ZigBee, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and others. ZigBee bulbs utilize low-power mesh networking for seamless smart home integration, ideal for larger installations. Wi-Fi bulbs connect directly to home networks, offering easy setup and remote control, but can consume more power. Bluetooth bulbs enable short-range connectivity, allowing local control without a hub, though with limited range. Other types may include proprietary protocols or newer technologies, catering to diverse consumer preferences and connectivity needs.</p></p>
