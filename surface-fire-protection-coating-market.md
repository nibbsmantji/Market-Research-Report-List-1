<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Coatings</li><li>Water-Based Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface fire protection coating market comprises three main types: solvent-based coatings, water-based coatings, and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings, known for their strong adhesion and durability, offer excellent fire resistance but can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, low in VOCs, and easier to apply, making them increasingly popular. Powder coatings, applied as a dry powder and cured under heat, provide a robust and uniform finish while being free from solvents, offering enhanced safety and environmental benefits.</p></p>
