Yaw rate sensors measure the vehicle's rotation around its vertical axis, essential for stability management. Lateral acceleration sensors gauge side-to-side forces, crucial for understanding vehicle dynamics during cornering. Together, these sensors contribute to enhanced safety and performance.</p></p>
