<p><p>The stainless steel resistors market is categorized into linear and non-linear resistors. Linear resistors maintain a constant resistance across varying voltage levels, ensuring predictable performance in applications such as circuit protection and signal processing. In contrast, non-linear resistors exhibit varying resistance based on voltage or current changes, making them ideal for applications like temperature sensing and surge protection. Both types are favored in industries requiring corrosion resistance, durability, and reliability, particularly in harsh environments.</p></p>
