<p><p>UV-sensitive resin 3D printers use ultraviolet light to cure liquid resin into solid objects. The main types include SLA (Stereolithography), which utilizes a laser to precisely harden resin layer by layer, resulting in high detail and smooth finishes. DLP (Digital Light Processing) uses a digital light projector to cure the resin, enabling faster printing speeds with good resolution. Other technologies in this market may include LCD-based printers, which offer a balance between cost and quality, catering to various user needs.</p></p>
