<p><p>Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) scanners are categorized into two main types based on their reading capabilities: Single Side Reading Type and Dual Side Reading Type. Single Side Reading Type scanners process marks on one side of a page, making them suitable for applications like surveys and tests where only one side is utilized. In contrast, Dual Side Reading Type scanners can read both sides of a sheet simultaneously, increasing efficiency and making them ideal for applications requiring comprehensive data capture, such as ballots and complex forms.</p></p>
