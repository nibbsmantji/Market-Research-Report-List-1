<p><p>The high-temperature resin market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Phenolic** resins offer excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, suitable for aerospace applications. </p><p>2. **Epoxy** resins are versatile, known for their strong adhesion and durability. </p><p>3. **Thermoplastics** are processable at high temperatures, providing flexibility and reusability. </p><p>4. **Polyimide** resins excel in extreme heat resistance, often used in electronics. </p><p>5. **Benzoxazine** typifies low shrinkage and good thermal properties. </p><p>6. **Cyanate ester** resins provide low moisture absorption and outstanding thermal stability, essential in high-performance applications.</p></p>
