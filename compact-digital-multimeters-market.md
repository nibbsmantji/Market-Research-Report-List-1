<p><p>The Compact Digital Multimeters market is categorized into Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel types. Single Channel multimeters measure one electrical parameter at a time, making them simple and cost-effective for basic tasks. Dual Channel devices can monitor two parameters simultaneously, enhancing versatility for troubleshooting and analysis. Multi Channel multimeters are advanced tools capable of measuring multiple parameters concurrently, ideal for complex applications in labs and industrial settings, providing comprehensive data collection and analysis in real-time.</p></p>
