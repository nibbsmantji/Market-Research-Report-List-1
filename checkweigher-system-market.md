<p><p>The checkweigher system market includes two primary types: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers continuously weigh products as they move along a production line, ensuring rapid, high-throughput processing suitable for fast-paced environments. Conversely, intermittent checkweighers pause the product during the weighing process, providing detailed measurements for items in slower, more controlled settings. Both types are essential for maintaining product quality, regulatory compliance, and reducing waste in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
