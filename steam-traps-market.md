<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trap</li><li>Thermostatic Trap</li><li>Thermodynamic Trap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam traps market comprises three main types: mechanical traps, thermostatic traps, and thermodynamic traps. Mechanical traps utilize physical movement to discharge condensate, making them ideal for varying pressure systems. Thermostatic traps respond to temperature changes, effectively managing condensate based on steam conditions, while thermodynamic traps operate on the principle of pressure differences, ensuring efficient condensate removal. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing system efficiency, energy conservation, and maintenance in industries reliant on steam systems.</p></p>
