<p><strong>The Inhalant Allergy Test Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total IgE</li><li>Specific IgE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inhalant Allergy Test Panel market primarily consists of two types: Total IgE and Specific IgE testing. Total IgE tests measure the overall level of immunoglobulin E antibodies in the blood, indicating a potential allergic response in the body. In contrast, Specific IgE testing identifies particular allergens responsible for triggering allergic reactions by measuring IgE antibodies against specific substances, such as pollen or pet dander. Both tests are crucial for accurate allergy diagnosis and management.</p></p>
