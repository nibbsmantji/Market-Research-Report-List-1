<p><p>Diamond abrasive blades are categorized based on their manufacturing techniques, primarily including sintering, high-frequency welding, and laser welding. Sintering involves compacting diamond particles and a metal matrix under heat, creating durable blades for cutting hard materials. High-frequency welding fuses diamond segments to a steel core using electromagnetic energy, ensuring a strong bond ideal for precision cutting. Laser welding employs focused laser beams to join segments to the core, providing precise and efficient production, catering to varying industrial needs.</p></p>
