<p><ul><li>Term Child Life Insurance</li><li>Permanent Child Life Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The juvenile insurance market includes two primary types: Term Child Life Insurance and Permanent Child Life Insurance. Term Child Life Insurance offers coverage for a specified period, typically providing a death benefit if the insured child passes away during the term. It is often more affordable but does not accumulate cash value. In contrast, Permanent Child Life Insurance provides lifelong coverage, builds cash value over time, and can serve as a long-term financial asset for the child’s future.</p></p>
