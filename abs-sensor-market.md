<p><strong>The ABS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS sensor market can be categorized into two primary types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to produce voltage changes, which are proportional to wheel speed, providing accurate data for anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors rely on changes in magnetic flux to generate signals based on wheel rotations. Both types are essential in enhancing vehicle safety and performance, ensuring efficient braking and stability control across various automotive applications.</p></p>
