<p><p>The GPS vehicle trackers market can be categorized into two main types: wired and battery-operated trackers. Wired trackers are directly connected to the vehicle's electrical system, providing constant power and often allowing for more advanced features and uninterrupted performance. In contrast, battery-operated trackers are self-contained units that rely on battery power, making them easy to install and ideal for temporary or covert tracking. Both types serve to enhance vehicle security, monitor fleet movements, and ensure efficient logistics management.</p></p>
