<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Monitoring (DAM) systems utilize advanced sensing technology to capture sound waves in various forms, primarily focusing on Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Monitoring and Transverse Wave Acoustic Monitoring. Longitudinal wave monitoring detects pressure changes along a medium, making it effective for applications like pipeline monitoring and geophysical surveys. In contrast, transverse wave monitoring detects shear waves, often used in structural health monitoring and seismic studies. Both types enhance real-time data collection for improved safety and operational efficiency across sectors.</p></p>
