<p><p>The caulking and sealant guns market comprises three main types: manual, electric, and pneumatic. Manual guns rely on hand pressure for application, providing control and versatility for small projects. Electric guns use a motor to deliver sealant efficiently, minimizing strain on the user and ensuring consistent flow for larger tasks. Pneumatic guns are powered by compressed air, offering high-speed application for industrial or extensive use, making them ideal for professionals seeking rapid and precise sealing solutions.</p></p>
