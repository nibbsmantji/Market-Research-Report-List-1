Downhole equipment includes tools designed for use inside wells, such as measuring instruments and sensors that collect data about geological formations and fluid properties. Surface equipment refers to tools and devices used above ground, facilitating data processing, analysis, and visualization. Together, these equipment types enable operators to obtain critical information for optimizing oil and gas exploration and production activities.</p></p>
