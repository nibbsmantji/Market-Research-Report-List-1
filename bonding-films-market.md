<p><p>Bonding films are adhesive materials used to join substrates, available in various curing types. Thermally cured films require heat to activate and solidify, offering strong, durable bonds ideal for high-temperature applications. Pressure cured films bond under applied pressure, suitable for delicate or heat-sensitive materials. Other types include chemically cured films, which harden through chemical reactions, and light-cured films, activated by UV or visible light, providing rapid curing and precision. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing performance, speed, and material compatibility.</p></p>
