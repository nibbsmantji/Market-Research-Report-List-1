<p><strong>The Audio Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Transformer</li><li>Input Transformer</li><li>Interstage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio transformers market is categorized into three main types: output transformers, input transformers, and interstage transformers. Output transformers convert high-voltage audio signals from tube amplifiers to low-impedance signals, optimizing sound quality. Input transformers serve to isolate and balance incoming signals, ensuring minimal noise and interference while enhancing the audio input. Interstage transformers are used between amplification stages to transfer signals, providing impedance matching and improving overall circuit performance. Each type plays a critical role in high-fidelity audio applications.</p></p>
