<p><ul><li>Linear Scales</li><li>Partial Arc Scales</li><li>Rotary Scales</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoders scale market encompasses three primary types: linear scales, partial arc scales, and rotary scales. Linear scales are used for precise position measurement along a straight path, while partial arc scales measure angles over a specific segment of a circle, ideal for applications requiring limited rotation. Rotary scales, on the other hand, provide full rotational position feedback, commonly utilized in motors and robotics. These scales enhance accuracy and performance in various industrial and automation applications.</p></p>
