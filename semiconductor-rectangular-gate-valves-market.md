<p><p>The semiconductor rectangular gate valves market is categorized into One Cylinder and Double Cylinder types based on their operational design. One Cylinder valves utilize a single actuator to control the flow, offering simplicity and cost efficiency, suitable for low-pressure applications. In contrast, Double Cylinder valves employ two actuators, allowing for greater control and reliability under higher pressures, making them ideal for more demanding environments. Both types are essential for maintaining precision in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
