<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) curing technology encompasses several market types: Mercury UV lamps, Metal Halide lamps, and LED UV curing. Mercury UV lamps are traditional, offering high-intensity UV light suitable for rapid curing of inks and coatings. Metal Halide lamps provide broad-spectrum UV light with improved efficiency but can be less versatile. LED UV curing, gaining popularity, is energy-efficient, has a long lifespan, and enables instant curing with lower heat emission. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
