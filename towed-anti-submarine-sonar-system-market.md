<p><p>The Towed Anti-Submarine Sonar System market is categorized into passive and active sensor types. Passive sensors detect submarines by listening for sounds they produce, such as engine noise and cavitation, allowing for stealthy operations. In contrast, active sensors emit sound waves and analyze their echoes to identify and locate submarines, offering a more precise tracking capability. Both systems play crucial roles in naval operations, enhancing maritime security by improving detection, tracking, and engagement of potential underwater threats.</p></p>
