<p><p>A bimetallic thermometer is a temperature measuring device that utilizes two different metals bonded together, which expand at different rates as they heat up. This differential expansion causes the metals to bend, moving a pointer on a dial to indicate the temperature. These thermometers are widely used in industrial applications for their durability, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The Bimetallic Thermometer Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. Moreover, there is a growing emphasis on safety and regulation in industrial processes, further boosting the need for precise temperature measurement.
