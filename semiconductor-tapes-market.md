<p><p>The semiconductor tapes market consists of specialized adhesives used in semiconductor fabrications, primarily including back grinding tapes and dicing tapes. Back grinding tapes are employed to protect semiconductor wafers during the thinning process, ensuring minimal damage and maintaining integrity. Dicing tapes, on the other hand, are used for holding wafers in place during the cutting process, aiding in the precise separation of individual chips. Both types are essential for enhancing manufacturing efficiency and quality in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
