<p><ul><li>Organic Hard Mask Material</li><li>Inorganic Hard Mask Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hard mask material market is divided into two primary types: organic hard mask materials and inorganic hard mask materials. Organic hard masks typically comprise polymers that provide excellent etch resistance and are used for precision patterning in semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, inorganic hard masks consist of materials like silicon dioxide and silicon nitride, offering superior thermal stability and durability. Both types serve critical roles in photolithography processes, ensuring high-resolution patterning and protection of underlying layers during etching.</p></p>
