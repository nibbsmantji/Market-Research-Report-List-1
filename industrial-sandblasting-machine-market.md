<p><strong>The Industrial Sandblasting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity feed Sandblaster</li><li>Pressure Sandblaster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial sandblasting machine market features two primary types: gravity feed sandblasters and pressure sandblasters. Gravity feed sandblasters utilize gravity to draw abrasive materials from a hopper to the nozzle, providing a consistent blast for lighter tasks. In contrast, pressure sandblasters store abrasive materials under pressure, allowing for a more powerful and adjustable flow of abrasives, ideal for heavier cleaning and surface preparation. Each type caters to different applications in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
