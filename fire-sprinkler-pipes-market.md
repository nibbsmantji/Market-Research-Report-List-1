<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Pipes Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe Systems</li><li>Dry Pipe Systems</li><li>Deluge Systems</li><li>Pre-Action Systems</li><li>Foam Water Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Water Spray Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire sprinkler pipes market encompasses various systems designed for fire protection. Wet Pipe Systems use water-filled pipes that discharge immediately upon heat detection. Dry Pipe Systems contain pressurized air, releasing water when a fire triggers the system, ideal for freezing environments. Deluge Systems flood entire areas with water through open sprinklers, suitable for high-hazard zones. Pre-Action Systems combine dry and wet features, requiring dual triggers for activation, minimizing accidental discharges. Foam Water Sprinkler Systems mix water with foam for flammable liquid fires. Water Spray Systems deliver fine water droplets for localized protection in industrial settings.</p></p>
