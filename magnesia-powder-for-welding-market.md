<p><p>Magnesia powder for welding is categorized into several types based on its chemical composition. MgO (magnesium oxide) is primarily used for its high melting point and thermal stability. Low iron variants minimize contamination during welding. Calcium-containing magnesia enhances fluidity in slag and improves weld quality. Sulfur-adjusted types act as deoxidizers, promoting cleaner welds by reducing oxides. Other specialty formulations may include additives tailored for specific applications or performance characteristics, ensuring versatility in welding processes across various industries.</p></p>
