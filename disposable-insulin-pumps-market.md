<p><p>The disposable insulin pumps market is divided into closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop systems, often referred to as an artificial pancreas, automate insulin delivery based on continuous glucose monitoring, adjusting doses in real-time to maintain optimal blood sugar levels. In contrast, open-loop systems require users to manually administer insulin based on their glucose readings and dietary needs. Both types cater to different patient preferences and management styles, impacting their adoption and market growth.</p></p>
