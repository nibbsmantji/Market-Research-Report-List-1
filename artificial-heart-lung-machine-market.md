<p><p>The artificial heart-lung machine market comprises two primary types: single roller pumps and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps utilize a single roller mechanism to move blood through the circuit, offering simplicity and reduced complexity. In contrast, double roller pumps employ two roller mechanisms, enhancing blood flow control and providing more consistent delivery, essential during intricate surgical procedures. Both types are crucial in supporting patients undergoing cardiothoracic surgeries, with their selection depending on specific clinical requirements and procedures.</p></p>
