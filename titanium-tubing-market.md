<p><p>The titanium tubing market includes straight tubes and U-bent tubes, both widely used in industries like aerospace, chemical processing, and power generation. Straight tubes are seamless or welded, offering high strength, corrosion resistance, and durability for applications like heat exchangers and pipelines. U-bent tubes, shaped into a "U" form, are ideal for compact heat exchangers and condensers, providing efficient heat transfer in limited spaces. Both types cater to demanding environments, ensuring reliability and performance in high-temperature and corrosive conditions.</p></p>
