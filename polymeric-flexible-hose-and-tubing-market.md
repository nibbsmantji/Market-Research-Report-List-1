<p><p>The polymeric flexible hose and tubing market includes three primary types: Thermosetting elastomers, which provide high heat resistance and durability, suitable for applications like automotive and industrial uses; Thermoplastic resins (non-elastomeric), known for their rigidity and strength, commonly used in applications requiring chemical resistance; and Thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), which combine the flexibility of elastomers with the processing advantages of thermoplastics, making them ideal for applications that require flexibility, chemical resistance, and easy processing.</p></p>
