<p><p>Data Loss Prevention (DLP) services are categorized into three main types: Network DLP, Endpoint DLP, and Storage/Data Center DLP. Network DLP focuses on monitoring and controlling data in transit across networks, preventing unauthorized data transfers. Endpoint DLP protects data on individual devices by managing access and usage policies. Storage/Data Center DLP safeguards sensitive information stored in databases and servers, ensuring compliance and preventing data breaches. Together, these solutions help organizations mitigate risks associated with data exposure and loss.</p></p>
