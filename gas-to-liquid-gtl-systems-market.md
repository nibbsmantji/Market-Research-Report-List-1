<p><p>Gas to Liquid (GTL) systems convert natural gas into liquid fuels through various processes. The Methanol to Gasoline (MTG) technique transforms methanol into gasoline, enhancing fuel quality. Fischer-Tropsch (FT) synthesis involves converting syngas, a mixture of hydrogen and carbon monoxide, into liquid hydrocarbons, producing high-quality diesel and lubricants. The Syngas to Gasoline process also utilizes syngas to create gasoline, focusing on efficient catalytic reactions. Each method offers unique benefits for producing cleaner, more sustainable liquid fuels from natural gas resources.</p></p>
