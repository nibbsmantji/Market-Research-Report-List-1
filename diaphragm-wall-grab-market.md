<p><p>The Diaphragm Wall Grab market comprises two main types: Hydraulic Diaphragm Wall Grabs and Normal Diaphragm Wall Grabs. Hydraulic Diaphragm Wall Grabs utilize hydraulic systems to enhance their lifting capacity and efficiency, making them suitable for deep excavations and challenging soil conditions. In contrast, Normal Diaphragm Wall Grabs rely on mechanical means for operation, offering a simpler and often more cost-effective solution for less demanding applications. Both types are essential for constructing reinforced concrete diaphragm walls in various civil engineering projects.</p></p>
