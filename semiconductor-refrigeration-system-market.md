<p><ul><li>"Single Stage"</li><li>"Multistage"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor refrigeration system market comprises primarily two types: single stage and multistage systems. Single stage systems utilize one thermoelectric module to achieve cooling, suitable for applications requiring moderate temperature differences. They are simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, multistage systems feature multiple thermoelectric modules in series, allowing for deeper cooling capabilities and higher temperature differentials. While multistage systems are more complex and pricier, they are ideal for specialized applications like medical devices and laser cooling where lower temperatures are crucial.</p></p>
