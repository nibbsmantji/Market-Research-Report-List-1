<p><p>Ammonia synthesis catalysts are specialized materials used in the production of ammonia through the Haber-Bosch process, which combines nitrogen and hydrogen under high pressure and temperature. Ammonia is a key component in fertilizers, chemicals, and industrial applications, driving the demand for these catalysts.</p><p>The Ammonia Synthesis Catalyst Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
