<p><p>The Metal-Ceramic Package Shell market encompasses two primary types: Al2O3 HTCC (High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) and AlN (Aluminum Nitride) HTCC ceramic shells. Al2O3 shells are known for their excellent dielectric properties and thermal conductivity, making them suitable for various electronic applications. In contrast, AlN shells offer superior thermal conductivity and low expansion coefficients, enhancing heat dissipation in high-performance devices. Both types are essential for the packaging of semiconductors, ensuring reliability and durability in electronic components.</p></p>
