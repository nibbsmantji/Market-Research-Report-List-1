<p><strong>The Traffic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Loop</li><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Bending Plate</li><li>Image Sensor</li><li>Infrared Sensor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traffic sensor market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Inductive Loop**: Embedded in the road, these sensors detect vehicle presence based on changes in magnetic fields.</p><p>2. **Piezoelectric Sensor**: Activated by vehicle pressure, they provide real-time data on traffic flow and weight.</p><p>3. **Bending Plate**: A metallic plate that bends under vehicle weight, triggering traffic counts.</p><p>4. **Image Sensor**: Utilizes cameras to capture vehicle data and monitor traffic conditions.</p><p>5. **Infrared Sensor**: Measures heat or light emitted by vehicles to determine their presence.</p><p>6. **Other**: Includes emerging technologies like radar and acoustic sensors.</p></p>
