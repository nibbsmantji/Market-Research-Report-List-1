<p><p>Magnesium Oxide (CAS 1309-48-4) is an inorganic compound with various market types. Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM) is produced through high-temperature calcination, providing high thermal stability. Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM), obtained by melting magnesite, offers superior purity and crystallinity. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM) is generated by calcining magnesium hydroxide, yielding higher reactivity. Synthetic Magnesium Oxide is produced through chemical processes, allowing for tailored properties, making it suitable for diverse industrial applications, including refractory materials, agriculture, and environmental sectors.</p></p>
