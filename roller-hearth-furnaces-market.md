<p><p>Roller hearth furnaces are essential in heat treatment processes, categorized into continuous and semi-continuous markets. Continuous heat treatment involves a constant flow of materials through the furnace, optimizing efficiency and output for high-volume production, suitable for industries like automotive and aerospace. In contrast, semi-continuous heat treatment allows for batch processing alongside continuous operations, accommodating diverse production needs while managing smaller lot sizes. Both types enhance material properties through precise temperature control and uniform heating, tailored to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
