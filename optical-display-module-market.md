<p><p>The optical display module market comprises several key solutions. The Prism Scheme uses prisms to efficiently direct light for enhanced image clarity. The Curved Mirror (Worm Eye) Solution employs curved mirrors to expand the field of view, improving visual experience. The Birdbath Scheme utilizes a unique reflective design to create immersive displays. Lastly, the Optical Waveguide Solution relies on waveguide technology to manipulate light for compact and versatile imaging, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics and augmented reality.</p></p>
