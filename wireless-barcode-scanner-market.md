<p><p>The wireless barcode scanner market can be divided into two main types: Wi-Fi-based and cellular-based scanners. Wi-Fi-based scanners connect to local networks, enabling real-time data transmission within a facility, making them ideal for inventory management in warehouses and retail environments. Conversely, cellular-based scanners utilize mobile networks, providing greater flexibility and mobility, allowing users to scan barcodes from remote locations or while on the move. Both types enhance operational efficiency but serve different connectivity needs and environments.</p></p>
