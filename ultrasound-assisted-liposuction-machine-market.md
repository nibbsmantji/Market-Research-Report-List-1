<p><p>The Ultrasound Assisted Liposuction (UAL) market is segmented into internal and external types. Internal UAL involves the insertion of a specialized cannula that emits ultrasound energy directly into the targeted fat cells, helping to liquefy the fat for easier removal. External UAL, on the other hand, utilizes ultrasound waves applied to the skin's surface, enhancing the emulsification of fat from a distance without direct insertion. Both methods improve precision and efficacy in fat reduction procedures, offering varying degrees of invasiveness.</p></p>
